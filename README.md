# Bubble Girl

Crash-style casino mini-game: place a bet, hold to inflate the bubble, cash out before it bursts.

## Play

Open `index.html` locally, or visit the Vercel deployment.

## Controls

- **New Round** — place bet and start
- **Hold** on the canvas — inflate multiplier (up to 4x)
- **Release / Cash Out** — lock in winnings
- Hold too long — bust and lose the bet
